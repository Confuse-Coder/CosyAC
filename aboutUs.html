HISTORY

The history of LG Electronics has always been surrounded by the company's desire to create a happier, better life.

Pioneering in technology through LG's innovative start and continuous growth, LG is becoming a faster and smarter global brand of the future.

Cosy Air Conditioners was established in 2020 and has since led the way into the advanced digital era thanks to the technological expertise acquired by manufacturing many home appliances such as radios and TVs.

Cosy Air Conditioners has unveiled many new products, applied new technologies in the form of mobile devices and digital Air Conditioners in the 21st century and continues to reinforce its status as a global company.

Code of Ethics

It is the intention of Cosy, as set forth in the management charter, to spread the management philosophies of "creating value for customers" and "respect based management" allowing for more responsible and open management. Cosy continues to develop as the global leading company through seeking greater public benefits founded in cooperation, mutual trust, and respect for the free market economy. For this reason all Cosy employees promise to act uprightly and make value judgments in accordance with the code of ethics..

Compliance of Conduct

Cosy Electronics is committed to "winning by the rules". We regard sound and transparent management practices as the foundation to achieving individual success and global competitiveness. Throughout our global operations, Cosy Electronics pledges to comply with local laws and regulations, compete fairly, and uphold the highest standards of business conduct. The Cosy Electronics Compliance Code of Conduct sets forth clear standards for legal and regulatory compliance all employees should live by in order to realize the principles provided in the Cosy Code of Ethics.
